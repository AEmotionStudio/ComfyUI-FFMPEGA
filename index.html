Features

Everything you need to edit video — powered by AI.

No FFMPEG knowledge needed. Describe what you want, and the agent handles the rest.

🗣️

Natural Language Editing

Type "make it cinematic with a vignette" or "speed up 2x, keep audio pitch" — the AI maps your words to precise FFMPEG filters.

🎭

SAM3 Auto-Mask

Describe what to remove — "remove the person" — and Segment Anything Model 3 generates per-frame masks with LaMa AI inpainting.

🤖

Multi-LLM Support

Works with Ollama (free local), OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini, Gemini CLI, Claude CLI, Cursor Agent, and Qwen CLI.

⚡

Programmatic Tool Calling

LLMs write a single Python script that orchestrates multiple tool calls in one pass — reducing 6 round-trips to 1.

🎙️

Whisper Auto-Transcribe

Transcribe audio with OpenAI Whisper and burn subtitles directly into video. Word-by-word karaoke mode included.

👁️

AI Vision System

The agent extracts frames and "sees" your video — analyzing colors, composition, and motion to make intelligent editing decisions.

How It Works

Your words become FFMPEG commands.

The AI agent analyzes your video, interprets your natural language instruction, selects the right skills from 200+ options, resolves parameter values, and composes a complete FFMPEG pipeline — with automatic error correction and output verification.

1. You describe the edit in plain English
2. AI selects skills, resolves parameters
3. Pipeline composed, dry-run validated
4. Output verified — auto-corrects if needed
